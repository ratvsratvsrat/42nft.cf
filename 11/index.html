REVOLUTION of CALLIGRAPHY - 42 Artists Collaboration

In this artwork, I reflected the impact of new technologies on humanity and, separately, on art and modern calligraphy.
Thanks to Crypto Art, we, artists, can now forever leave our imprint in time in the form of our works of art. This imposes on us a certain responsibility before taking a step into the future.
We are on the verge of crazy discoveries. Technology is advancing at such a speed that it is not known what will happen tomorrow.
What will it be tomorrow?
We must answer this question today. The qualities that we possess today determine the qualities of the whole world tomorrow. And one of these qualities should be...
LOVE
Love for people, love for the world, love for life, love for your work.
By changing our thoughts today, we are changing tomorrow's world around us.
